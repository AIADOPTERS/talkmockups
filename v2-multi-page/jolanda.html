Meet JoLanda

Photo of JoLanda

I’m JoLanda Rogers

For over 15 years, I’ve walked with individuals, leaders, ministries, and organizations as they heal, grow, and rediscover who they are. My work sits at the intersection of clinical psychotherapy and business strategy, because I believe healing and growth were never meant to be separate journeys.

I created the T.A.L.K.™ Experience because I kept meeting brilliant, exhausted people who were trying to build something meaningful while running on empty. They needed a place where truth meets transformation.

Your healing matters. Your story matters. Your voice matters.
  • Licensed Psychotherapist specializing in trauma, anxiety, and identity work
  • Business Therapist and Strategist for entrepreneurs and leaders
  • Author and founder of My Author Lab
  • Speaker and workshop facilitator on healing-centered leadership
  • Creator of Journey On Demand, Sweet Girl Heal, and Heal Your Business Academy
