I’m JoLanda Rogers

The Heart Behind The T.A.L.K.™ Brand

Licensed Psychotherapist, Business Therapist, Strategist, Author, and the heart behind the T.A.L.K. brand.

For over 15 years, I’ve walked with individuals, leaders, ministries, and organizations as they heal, grow, and rediscover who they are beneath the overwhelm, the trauma, the pressure, and the expectations. My work lives at the intersection of mental health, soul healing, business strategy, leadership, and storytelling, because your life doesn’t exist in silos, and your healing shouldn’t either.

Through clinical expertise, spiritual depth, compassion, and strategic clarity, I help people:

  • find relief and grounding
  • process trauma and navigate emotional pain
  • reconnect with identity and purpose
  • build and lead from a healed place
  • create soul-aligned businesses
  • share the stories they’ve carried for years
  • rise with confidence in their calling

Whether through therapy, coaching, writing, leadership development, or organizational consulting, my mission remains the same: to help you heal your soul, transform your story, and live the life you were created for.

Your healing matters. Your story matters. Your voice matters. And there is space for all of you here.
